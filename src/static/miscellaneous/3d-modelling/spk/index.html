SPK

A total conversion created during my first year of university.

You follow a randomly generated path through space, with randomly chosen waves of ships attacking.

Some design aspects were a little unusual, such as bullets following the curve of the level, and the ability to slow down, which means you encounter more ships during the level, but have more time to deal with them.
